This is a diagram showing IMDB rating for each Adventure Time episode in first seven seasons. Rating is expressed relative to the median IMDB rating for all Adventure Time episodes.

Bars on the left side indicate rating is lower than the median, and bars on the right indicate that rating is higher than the median. Clicking on a bar will lead you to the IMDB page for that episode. Visualization was built using D3.js and data was scraped with Nokogiri.

I had fun building this so enjoy it and stay mathematical!
